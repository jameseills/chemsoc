ChemSoc

Southampton University's Student Chemistry Society

Who are we?

We are a student-run society for Chemistry at Southampton University, comprising the Committee, undergraduate and post-gradiuate students. That's currently 1000 members.

How do I join?

We contact all Freshers during their first week at Southampton. If you are already here and not yet a member, you can: Join ChemSoc Today

What do we do?

As outlined in our Constitution, we:

  • provide information to Chemistry students
  • organise Social events
  • maintain a Careers resource
